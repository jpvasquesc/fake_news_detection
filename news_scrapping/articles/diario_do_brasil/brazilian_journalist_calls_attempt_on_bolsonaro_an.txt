Brazilian journalist calls attempt on Bolsonaro an “unsuccessful stab”
When commenting on the attack suffered by Jair Bolsonaro during the presidential campaign, the … sic … journalist Rachel Sheherazade caused revolt among Internet users.
In an interview with Minister Gilmar Mendes, she lists the events that would have contributed to Bolsonaro’s election, among them the “unsuccessful stab” delivered by Adélio Bispo.
“The Lava-Jato and the arrest of former President Lula had a decisive impact on the 2018 elections. Not only that, but the unsuccessful stabbing of Adélio Bispo, who also ran for Bolsonaro’s election” – fired Sheherazade.
